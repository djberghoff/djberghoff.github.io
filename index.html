About Me

I am a senior at Ohio State University studying Geographic Information Science (GIS). I am interested in how visual representation of spatial data can relate to a broad audience to solve problems. My previous work has included projects in environmental science, planning, business development, sustainability, and human geography. When I am not mapping, I enjoy playing basketball, hiking, camping, and eating good food wherever I go.

This webpage serves as a portfolio for all Geography 5201 assignments during the Spring 2015 semester.
